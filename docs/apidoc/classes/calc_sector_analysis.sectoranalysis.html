getPartition

  • getPartition(): Promise<{ direct: number[]; totals: number[]; upstream: number[] }>

  • A partition contains the indicator results of a sector analysis related to 1 USD output broken into a part that is related to the direct operations of a sector and a part that is related to the upstream (supply chain) of that sector.

    Returns Promise<{ direct: number[]; totals: number[]; upstream: number[] }>
